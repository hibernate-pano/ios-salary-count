# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**牛马薪水计算器 (SalaryCount)**
最后更新 / Last updated: 2026-06-06

---

## 中文

### 我们收集什么数据？

**我们不收集任何数据。** 牛马薪水计算器不收集、不上传、不分享你的任何个人信息。

### 你的数据存在哪里？

你在 App 中输入的所有信息（月薪、上下班时间、午休、工作日、主题偏好等）**仅保存在你的设备本地**。这些数据：

- 不会离开你的设备
- 不会上传到任何服务器
- 不会与任何第三方共享
- 我们（开发者）无法访问

如果你使用桌面小组件，配置会通过 Apple 的 App Group 机制在你设备的 App 与小组件之间共享，同样仅限本地。

### 网络与第三方

本 App **完全离线运行**，不发起任何网络请求，不集成任何分析、广告或追踪 SDK。法定节假日数据为 App 内置，无需联网获取。

### 你的控制权

你的数据完全由你掌控。卸载 App 即可彻底删除设备上的所有相关数据。

### 儿童隐私

本 App 不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童信息（实际上不收集任何人的信息）。

### 联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 App Store 页面的开发者联系方式与我们联系。

### 政策变更

若本政策有更新，我们会在此页面更新「最后更新」日期。

## English

### What data do we collect?

**We collect no data.** SalaryCount does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information.

### Where is your data stored?

Everything you enter in the app (monthly salary, work hours, lunch break, work days, theme preferences, etc.) is **stored only locally on your device**. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is never uploaded to any server
- Is never shared with any third party
- Cannot be accessed by us (the developer)

If you use the home screen widget, your configuration is shared between the app and the widget via Apple's App Group mechanism, which is also entirely local to your device.

### Network & Third Parties

This app runs **fully offline**. It makes no network requests and integrates no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Public holiday data is bundled within the app and requires no internet connection.

### Your Control

You are in full control of your data. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all related data from your device.

### Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, nor does it knowingly collect information from children (in fact, it collects no information from anyone).

### Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the developer contact information on our App Store page.

### Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page.
